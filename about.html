About us

A place for learning,discovery,discipline and security

Our Mission

Our mission is to make the Institution, Best in the Nation and To promote a safe, orderly, caring and supportive learning environment.We strongly beleive that Discipline is necessary to suceed in Life as said by our Founder Dr. Jeppiar


We inculcate a strong sense of discipline and fine behaviour among the students and enable the students for achieving proficiency in engineering to attain the highest altitude in their career.



Panimalar Polytechnic college is a Christian Minority Institution of higher education meant for aspiring the youth, and comes under the direction of the JaiSakthi Educational Trust. The College, an ISO certified institution located in Nelson Manickam Road, Nungambakkam, inculates a spirit of learning and discipline in a campus that offers high class facilities to its students.

The JaiSakthi Educational Trust strives to impart quality education in engineering, science & technology to the aspiring youth of India in a disciplined manner. This institution meets all the criteria set by the All India Council for Technical Education and has not applied for Accreditation by NBA.

It ensures that an all round development is imparted to every student who enters the portals of the Panimalar college, for sufficient focus is given to home their communication and analytical skills. The college hosts the best infrastructure, thus making the campus life both an educative and enjoyable one for the student.

This sprawling Panimalar campus provides a holistic education in an ambience that makes no compromises on discipline, dedication and commitment. The result of which, students who pass out of the college turn out to be academically brilliant, morally upright and empowered individuals.

Vision

  • To set a high level of moral and professional ethics in Engineering Education.
  • To raise the standard of Technical Education.
  • To facilitate the well-being of the community at large.
  • To create an enlightened professionals for facing the challenges of this modern scientific world.
