National Service Scheme

NSS is a Two Fold program consisting of regular activities and special camping program. A variety of programs are undertaken by the NSS unit as suggested by the Government from time to time. NSS unit may select the program as per the need of the area, means, resources and skills.

Our NSS units have undertaken several programs like planting of trees, Digging canals, Family welfare, Nutrition Program, Adults Education and Mass Literacy programs successfully. Some of the mass programs such as "Blood Donation", "Eye Camps" and "Health Camps" have also been successfully organized by our NSS units joining hands with Social organizations like Rotary Club, Lions Club etc.

NSS special camps are organized every year in rural areas and small villages, for the benefit of the villagers. The NSS volunteers are also trained in personality development, character building and leadership qualities through orientation programs.
