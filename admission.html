Eligibility

As per New Government Order, Eligible Marks for Joining Polytechnic Course (Both I Year & Direct II Year) for all communities are "JUST PASS" in S.S.L.C (with Maths and Science Compulsory) / H.Sc (With Maths, Physics & Chemistry Compulsory) Examination or Equivalent Examination.


The following ORIGINAL Certificates should be produced to the Principal at the time of admission :

  • S.S.L.C I H.Sc Mar Sheet
  • Transfer Certificates
  • Conduct Certificate
  • Community Certificate
  • Recent Passport size Photo - 3 Nos
  • For other State I Board Students should bring Nativity Certificate from Local Authorities.

All the above mentioned is Mandatory

Admission procedure

The completed application form with enclosures should be submitted to:

The Principal,

PANIMALAR POLYTECHNIC COLLEGE

No.23, Railway Colony II Street,

Nelson Manickam Road,

Chennai - 600 029.

A committee of experts shall scrutinize the applications and decide on the Candidates be called for personal interview.The Candidates should produce all the relevant Certificates in original at the time of INTERVIEW.

Selected Candidates must pay the fees in Cash or Demand Draft Drawn (Crossed Payee only) in favour of PANIMALAR POLYTECHNIC COLLEGE on any Nationalised Bank in Chennai as detailed in the admission letter on or before the date mentioned in. Failing, which the selection will automatically stand cancelled.
